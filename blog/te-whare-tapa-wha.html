Defining Te Whare Tapa Wha

Te Whare Tapa Wha is a concept used in Māori health and well-being. It compares a person's well-being to the four walls of a house. Each wall represents a different aspect of health.

The idea is that all these aspects need to be structured - strong and balanced, like the walls of a house, for a person to be truly healthy and well.
