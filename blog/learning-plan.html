Learning Plan Questions & Answers:

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

I chose to develop myself in the IT field because there is immense potential. To be honest, I have no 5 year plan. My future is very unclear to me - as for what I want to do with my life, I've got no idea. The catch is, I value freedom. I wanted to allow myself the grace & room to be able to decide as I go. I'm confident in my abilities to succeed in this industry, there's endless opportunity for up-skilling & space for growth. This is enough for me.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning:

My Strengths

  • Good at communicating. Wont hesitate to ask for help.
  • Good work ethic. I'm used to working hard.
  • Confident in my abilities. I have a strong will to succeed.

My limitations

  • Lack of prior knowledge. I'm a beginner.
  • Procrastination. I need a deadline to motivate my work output.
  • Tunnel-vision. When I'm in a good flow-state, it's hard for me to stop - to the point of over exhaustion.

What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to develop my tolerance towards other people. I have a tendency to get impatient with others, specifically when I feel there is a lack of self awareness, common sense or care.
